Top 300 Baseball Players: WAR Data Visualization

  1. Hover over a curve to see who the player is
  2. Hover over a dot to see that player's wins above k war value at that value of k
  3. Click on a curve to see that player highlighted in the table (you may need to scroll down in the table)
  4. Click on a row in the table to see that player's curve highlighted
  5. Check the hall of fame box to see all hall of fame players (players not in hall of fame fade out)
  6. Select a position from the dropdown to see all players that play that position (select the first blank option to deselect all positions)
  7. Search for or choose a player in the text field to highlight their curve
  8. Refresh the page to clear all highlighting
